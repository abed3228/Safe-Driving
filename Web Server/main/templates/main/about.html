    About Us

    We develop this system as part of my final project at the College of Management Academic Studies

    the project target:

    Vehicle alcohol test system before starting.
    If the level of alcohol is allowed, the system will not allow the driver to start the vehicle.
    In addition, the system sends an SMS message to the phone that is defined as a backup that includes
    the location (GPS) of the vehicle Otherwise the vehicle will start

    the projects description:

    Problem and solution of drunk driving

    Data from the oryarok Association, based on data from the Central Bureau of Statistics in Israel
    It appears that 279 road accidents with casualties occur every year on average,
    when the driver was under the influence of alcohol / drugs (years data 2006-2011)

    Data from the Traffic Division for 2019 in the last decade and with them an amazing figure: from 2009 to 2019
    Police arrested 106,121 drivers while intoxicated or under the influence of drugs
    According to the division's data, in targeted activity on the subject in 2019, 9,774 drivers were caught driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs,
    compared to last year, when 9,361 drivers were caught.
    An increase of more than 4% according to an article by Israel Today

    In addition even if you are a responsible driver how can you know that it consumed the amount of alcohol allowed by law
    A drunk driver is someone who drives under the influence of alcohol or an intoxicating or dangerous drug,
    when the concentration of alcohol in his body is 240 micrograms of alcohol per liter of
    exhaled air or more or 50 milligrams of alcohol per hundred milliliters of blood in a drunkenness or blood test.

    Therefore these drivers not only endanger themselves but also endanger the rest of the road users
    Therefore a solution is needed to prevent those drivers from getting on a vehicle and started driving
    As a result, the Safe Driving system will prevent a drunk driver from starting a vehicle and a passenger,
    thus endangering everyone

    The main goal of the Safe Driving system is to reduce drunk driving,
    so we have taken dangerous people off the road so we can save people.
